# 5-Year Program Plan (2025–2030)

**Focus:** Bulk + single-cell transcriptomics; **Cancer** & **Neurodegenerative diseases**  
**Stack:** R, Python, Linux (Snakemake; Conda/Mamba; Docker/Singularity)

## Vision & Outcomes
- Publish **≥ 50** papers (Nature-portfolio & others) using public data.
- Release **10+** open-source tools, **5+** benchmarks, **3+** reference datasets.
- Build reproducible pipelines for **bulk**, **single-cell**, and **spatial** data.

## Public Health Impact
This program accelerates biomarker discovery and risk stratification via **open data** and **open tools**, enabling adoption in **resource-limited settings**.

## Workstreams
T1 Pan-cancer (bulk) • T2 TME & Immuno-oncology • T3 Neurodegeneration • T4 Single-cell atlases & cross-modal AI • T5 Methods & Community
